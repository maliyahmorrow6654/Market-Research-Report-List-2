<p><ul><li>Conventional CMP Diamond Pad Disks</li><li>CVD Diamond CMP Pad Disks</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>CMP diamond disks are used in chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) processes to polish and flatten semiconductor wafers. Conventional CMP diamond pad disks are made from natural or synthetic diamond particles embedded in a polymer matrix, providing moderate polishing rates and high durability. On the other hand, CVD diamond CMP pad disks are produced using chemical vapor deposition techniques, resulting in higher quality diamond material with superior polishing performance. Both types of disks play a crucial role in achieving precise planarity and surface finish in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
