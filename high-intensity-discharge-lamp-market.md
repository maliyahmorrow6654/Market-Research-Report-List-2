<p><ul><li>Gas Discharge Lamp</li><li>High-Pressure Gas Discharge Lamp</li><li>Electrodeless Discharge Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-intensity discharge lamp market can be divided into three types: gas discharge lamp, high-pressure gas discharge lamp, and electrodeless discharge lamp. Gas discharge lamps use different noble gases and metal halides to produce light. High-pressure gas discharge lamps operate at higher pressures, leading to increased brightness and longer lifespan. Electrodeless discharge lamps utilize magnetic fields to induce light emission, eliminating the need for electrodes. These types offer varying features and benefits to cater to different lighting requirements.</p></p>
