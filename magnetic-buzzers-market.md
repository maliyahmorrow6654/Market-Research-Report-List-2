<p><strong>The Magnetic Buzzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Magnetic Buzzer</li><li>Passive Magnetic Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Buzzers market consists of two types: Active Magnetic Buzzers and Passive Magnetic Buzzers. Active Magnetic Buzzers are electronic devices that produce sound using an integrated oscillator circuit, allowing them to emit continuous or pulse sounds. They are commonly used in various applications, such as alarms and electronic devices. On the other hand, Passive Magnetic Buzzers do not have a built-in oscillator circuit and require an external signal to produce sound. They are utilized in situations where an external signal source is readily available, such as in automobiles and household appliances.</p></p>
