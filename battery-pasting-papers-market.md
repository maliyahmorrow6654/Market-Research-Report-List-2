<p><ul><li>Cellulose Pasting Papers</li><li>Fiberglass Pasting Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery pasting papers market comprises two main types: cellulose pasting papers and fiberglass pasting papers. Cellulose pasting papers are made from natural wood pulp and are widely used in battery manufacturing due to their excellent absorption properties and durability. On the other hand, fiberglass pasting papers are manufactured using fiberglass material, making them highly resistant to acid and temperature, thus suitable for demanding battery applications. Both types of pasting papers play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of batteries by providing electrode support and enhancing their overall performance.</p></p>
