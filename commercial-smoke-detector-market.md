<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The commercial smoke detector market consists of various types of detectors. Photoelectric smoke detectors use light sensors to detect smoldering fires and are more effective in detecting larger particles of smoke. Dual sensor detectors combine photoelectric and ionization technologies to detect both smoldering and fast-burning fires. Ionization smoke detectors use a small amount of radioactive material to detect fast-burning fires. Each type has its advantages and is used in different commercial settings based on the specific fire risks and safety requirements.</p></p>
