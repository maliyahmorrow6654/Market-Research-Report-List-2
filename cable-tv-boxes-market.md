<p><p>Cable TV boxes are electronic devices that receive and decode television signals for viewing on a television set. There are three main types of cable TV boxes in the market. Analog cable TV boxes receive and display television signals in an analog format, which provides lower image quality. HD digital cable TV boxes receive and display television signals in high-definition format, offering superior image and sound quality. Standard digital cable TV boxes receive and display television signals in a digital format, providing better clarity and audio compared to analog boxes, but not as high as HD digital.</p></p>
