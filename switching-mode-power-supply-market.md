<p><p>The switching mode power supply (SMPS) market can be broadly categorized into two types: AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC SMPS refers to power supplies that convert alternating current (AC) input into direct current (DC) output, commonly used in consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and communication devices. On the other hand, DC/DC SMPS are power supplies that convert one DC voltage level to another DC voltage level, typically used in automotive, aerospace, and renewable energy applications. These two market types cater to different industry needs and play a crucial role in providing efficient and reliable power solutions.</p></p>
