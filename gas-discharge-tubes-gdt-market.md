<p><p>Gas Discharge Tubes (GDT) are devices that protect electrical equipment from voltage surges and transient voltage events by quickly diverting excess current away from sensitive components. The market for GDT is categorized into three types: Glass Gas Discharge Tubes, Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes, and Others. Glass GDTs are commonly used due to their high insulation resistance and low breakdown voltage. Ceramic GDTs, on the other hand, offer enhanced durability and can operate in extreme temperature environments. The "Others" category includes specialty GDTs specifically designed for unique applications, such as those requiring high frequency or high surge current capabilities.</p></p>
