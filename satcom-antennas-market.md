<p><p>SATCOM antennas are used for satellite communication and are available in various frequency bands. The L Band offers reliable satellite communication for mobile applications such as maritime and aviation. The Ku Band provides high-speed data transmission and is commonly used for broadcasting satellite TV. The C Band is used for satellite communications in fixed locations due to its resistance to rain fade. The X band is mainly used for military and government applications. Additionally, there are other market segments that cater to specific frequency bands or hybrid solutions to meet diverse communication requirements.</p></p>
