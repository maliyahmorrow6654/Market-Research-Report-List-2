<p><p>LGP (Light Guide Plate) is a component used in the backlighting of LCD screens. There are two main types of LGP market: Printed LGP and Non-Printed LGP. Printed LGP refers to plates that have patterns or designs printed on them, usually using a screen printing process. This allows for customization and the creation of unique lighting effects. Non-Printed LGP, on the other hand, does not have any printed patterns and is typically used for uniform and even lighting applications. Both types serve the purpose of distributing light evenly across the LCD screen.</p></p>
