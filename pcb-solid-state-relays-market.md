The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.30% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the key trends driving the market growth is the rising adoption of SSRs in electric vehicle (EV) charging stations and renewable energy systems. These relays offer precise and accurate control of electrical loads, making them suitable for various automated systems. The ability of SSRs to operate in harsh environmental conditions and handle high-voltage and high-current loads also contributes to their growing popularity.</p><p>Another important aspect is the miniaturization of PCB SSRs, which allows for their integration into compact electronic devices and circuit boards.
