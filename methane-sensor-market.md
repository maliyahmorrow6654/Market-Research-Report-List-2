<p><p>The Methane Sensor Market is divided into two main types: Infrared Sensor and Catalytic Bead Sensor. Infrared Sensors detect methane gas by measuring the absorption of infrared light emitted by the gas. These sensors are highly accurate but often more expensive. Catalytic Bead Sensors, on the other hand, detect methane through a catalytic reaction. They are typically less expensive but may require regular calibration. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, such as oil and gas, agriculture, and environmental monitoring, ensuring safety and preventing potential hazards.</p></p>
