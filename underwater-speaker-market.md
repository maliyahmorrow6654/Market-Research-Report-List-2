<p><p>The underwater speaker market can be classified into three types: portable speakers, flush mount speakers, and floating-on-top speakers. Portable speakers are designed to be easily carried around and submerged in water, allowing users to enjoy music while swimming or diving. Flush mount speakers are installed in pools or water bodies to provide a discreet underwater audio experience. Floating-on-top speakers, as the name suggests, float on the water's surface, enabling individuals to listen to music while relaxing in the pool or any water environment. These speakers cater to different preferences and usage scenarios, ensuring a diverse range of options for consumers.</p></p>
