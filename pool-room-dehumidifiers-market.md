<p><p>Pool room dehumidifiers are devices used to control the humidity levels in pool rooms, which can become prone to excess moisture. There are two main types of dehumidifiers used for this purpose. Portable dehumidifiers are smaller units that can be easily moved around and placed wherever needed. They are suitable for smaller pool rooms or spaces with less humidity. On the other hand, whole-home dehumidifiers are larger units that are permanently installed in the pool room's HVAC system. They are designed to dehumidify the entire space efficiently, making them ideal for larger pool rooms or areas with high humidity levels.</p></p>
