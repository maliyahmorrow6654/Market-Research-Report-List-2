<p><p>The MEMS motion sensor market consists of three main types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers measure changes in velocity and detect motion in devices such as smartphones and gaming controllers. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity and rotational changes, enabling image stabilization in cameras and navigation in drones. Magnetometers measure magnetic fields, allowing compass functionality in smartphones and navigation systems. These motion sensors play a vital role in enhancing user experience and functionality across various electronic devices, driving the growth of the MEMS motion sensor market.</p></p>
