<p><p>The energy-efficient lamps market consists of various types of lamps, including fluorescent lamps, induction lamps, high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps, light emitting diode (LED) lamps, and others. Fluorescent lamps work by converting electrical energy into light using a gas-filled tube. Induction lamps utilize electromagnetic fields to generate light. HID lamps generate light by passing an electrical current through a gas or vapor. LED lamps produce light through the movement of electrons in a semiconductor material. The "others" category includes unconventional energy-efficient lamp types, such as halogen lamps or compact fluorescent lamps.</p></p>
