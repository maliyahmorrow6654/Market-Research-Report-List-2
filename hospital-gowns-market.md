<p><p>The hospital gowns market can be categorized into two types: disposable and reusable gowns. Disposable gowns are designed for single-use and are made of lightweight materials such as non-woven fabrics. They are convenient as they can be discarded after use, minimizing the risk of contamination. Reusable gowns, on the other hand, are made of more durable materials such as cotton or polyester blends, and can be washed and sterilized for multiple usages. They are cost-effective in the long-term and environmentally friendly, but require proper cleaning and maintenance protocols.</p></p>
