<p><p>FBG Interrogator Market offers two types of interrogators: 1x4 Type (4 Channels) and 1x8 Type (8 Channels). The 1x4 type comes with four channels, enabling it to monitor four Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensors simultaneously. Meanwhile, the 1x8 type expands this capability by allowing the user to monitor eight FBG sensors at once. These interrogators are used to measure various physical parameters like temperature and strain in sectors such as telecommunications, energy, and aerospace. They provide efficient and accurate data collection for real-time monitoring and analysis of FBG sensors.</p></p>
