<p><p>Li-ion batteries for electric buses come in different types, such as LFP and NMC. LFP (lithium iron phosphate) batteries provide enhanced safety features, longer cycle life, and better thermal stability, making them a reliable choice for electric buses. On the other hand, NMC (nickel-manganese-cobalt) batteries offer higher energy density and greater power capacity, making them suitable for buses requiring longer driving ranges or heavier loads. Both LFP and NMC batteries play a crucial role in advancing the electric bus industry by providing efficient and sustainable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
