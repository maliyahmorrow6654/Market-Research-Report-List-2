<p><p>The Avalanche Backpack market is classified into two types: Mono Avalanche Airbags and Dual Avalanche Airbags. Mono Avalanche Airbags are equipped with a single airbag system that inflates during an avalanche, providing buoyancy and helping the user stay on the surface of the snow. In contrast, Dual Avalanche Airbags feature two independent airbags to provide enhanced safety, as one can still function if the other is damaged. Both types of backpacks are crucial for backcountry activities, offering users protection and reducing the risk of burial in an avalanche.</p></p>
