<p><strong>The Si Epitaxial Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homoepitaxial Epitaxial Wafer</li><li>Heteroepitaxial Epitaxial Wafer</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Si epitaxial wafer market is divided into two types: homoepitaxial epitaxial wafers and heteroepitaxial epitaxial wafers. Homoepitaxial epitaxial wafers are made by growing a layer of silicon on top of a silicon substrate, resulting in a wafer with the same crystal structure as the substrate. On the other hand, heteroepitaxial epitaxial wafers involve growing a layer of silicon on a different material, such as silicon germanium or sapphire, resulting in a wafer with a different crystal structure than the substrate. These different types cater to diverse requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
