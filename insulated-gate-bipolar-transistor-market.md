<p><p>The Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) is a three-terminal power semiconductor device that is extensively used in various applications such as power electronics, renewable energy systems, consumer electronics, electric vehicles, and industrial automation, among others. The IGBT combines the strong current-handling capability of the bipolar junction transistor (BJT) with the high input impedance and voltage control of a metal oxide semiconductor field-effect transistor (MOSFET).</p><p>The growing need for energy-efficient devices and increasing demand for high-performance power modules has been driving the growth of the IGBT market.
