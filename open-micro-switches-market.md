<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Open micro switches are classified into three types based on their size and sealing capabilities. Sealed miniature switches are the largest and provide higher current ratings, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Sealed subminiature switches are smaller and have lower current ratings, commonly used in industrial and automation equipment. Sealed ultra subminiature switches are the smallest and have the lowest current ratings, suitable for applications with limited space. These switches are sealed to prevent dust, moisture, and other contaminants from affecting their performance, ensuring reliability and durability in various environments.</p></p>
