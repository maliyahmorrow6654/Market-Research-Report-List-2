<p><p>The Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) Battery market includes two major types: AGM Battery and GEL Battery. AGM Battery, also known as Absorbent Glass Mat Battery, utilizes a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, resulting in a safe and maintenance-free design. It offers high performance and is commonly used in UPS systems, automotive applications, and solar power storage. On the other hand, GEL Battery contains a silica gel that immobilizes the electrolyte, making it spill-proof and suitable for deep cycle applications. It has superior cycling capabilities and is commonly utilized in medical devices, wheelchairs, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
