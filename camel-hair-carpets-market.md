<p><p>Camel hair carpets are produced from the hair of either dromedary or bactrian camels, leading to two distinct market types. Dromedary camel hair carpets are derived from the hair of a single-humped Arabian or Somali camel, known for its fine and soft fibers. These carpets are highly valued for their luxurious texture and are commonly used in upscale decor. On the other hand, Bactrian camel hair carpets utilize the hair of a double-humped camel mainly found in Central Asia. These carpets are particularly sought-after for their durability and rugged appearance, suitable for both traditional and modern settings.</p></p>
