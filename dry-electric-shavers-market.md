<p><strong>The Dry Electric Shavers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Foil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry electric shavers come in two main types: rotary type and foil type. Rotary shavers have circular blades that move in a circular motion, allowing them to easily adjust to the contours of the face. They are suitable for longer hair and give a close shave. Foil shavers have blades covered by a thin metal foil with small holes that capture hairs for a clean cut. They work best for shorter hair and deliver a smooth shave. Both types are popular in the market and offer different features to satisfy users' preferences.</p></p>
