<p><p>The light field camera market consists of several types of cameras. Standard Plenoptic Camera captures the light field by recording the direction and intensity of rays through microlenses. Focused Plenoptic Camera uses a lens array to capture spatial and angular information of light. Coded Aperture Camera reconstructs the light field by applying a coded mask. Stereo With Plenoptic Camera combines stereo and plenoptic techniques to capture both depth and intensity information. These cameras are used in various applications such as virtual reality, cinematography, scientific imaging, and augmented reality.</p></p>
