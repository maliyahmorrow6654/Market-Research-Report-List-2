<p><p>Shock detectors are devices used to detect and measure shock or impact forces in various industries. There are several types of shock detectors available in the market. Piezoelectric shock detectors generate an electric charge when subjected to mechanical stress. Piezoresistive detectors change their electrical resistance based on the applied force. Capacitor-based detectors measure the change in capacitance resulting from the shock. Strain gage detectors detect and measure strain or deformation caused by the shock. Lastly, there are other types of shock detectors that use different mechanisms to detect and measure shock forces.</p></p>
