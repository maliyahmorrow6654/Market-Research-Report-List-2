<p><p>The lightweight foam boards market includes three main types: PVC foam boards, expanded polystyrene foam boards, and extruded polystyrene foam boards. PVC foam boards are made from a lightweight and rigid plastic material, widely used for signage, display boards, and construction purposes. Expanded polystyrene foam boards are created from expanded polystyrene beads, offering excellent insulation and being commonly employed in packaging, insulation, and arts and crafts. Lastly, extruded polystyrene foam boards are produced via an extrusion process, providing thermal insulation and frequently utilized in construction, insulation, and roofing applications.</p></p>
