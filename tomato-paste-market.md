<p><p>Tomato paste is available in two market types: natural and composite. Natural tomato paste is made solely from tomatoes without any added ingredients. It retains the pure taste and qualities of freshly harvested tomatoes. Composite tomato paste, on the other hand, is a blend of tomato puree with other ingredients like salt, sugar, and preservatives. This type of paste offers a more convenient and consistent flavor profile, catering to varied tastes and culinary preferences. The choice between the two market types depends on individual preferences, intended use, and specific recipe requirements.</p></p>
