<p><p>Cold-drawn hooked end steel fiber is a type of reinforcing material used in concrete structures to enhance their strength and durability. These fibers are made through a process of drawing steel through a die to achieve the desired shape and dimensions. The hooked end ensures better bonding with the concrete matrix, resulting in improved crack resistance and load-bearing capacity of the structure.</p><p>The global cold-drawn hooked end steel fiber market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The growing demand for high-performance concrete in various infrastructure projects, such as bridges, tunnels, and industrial flooring, is driving the market. Cold-drawn hooked end steel fibers offer excellent mechanical properties, such as high tensile strength and ductility, making them suitable for demanding applications in the construction industry.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing awareness regarding the advantages of using steel fibers over traditional reinforcement methods, such as rebar, is fueling the market growth.
