<p><p>Aircraft Interior Protective Films market encompasses various types of protective films used in the aviation industry. Epoxy films are known for their high strength and flexibility, making them ideal for protecting aircraft interiors from scratches and damage. PVF (Polyvinyl fluoride) films offer excellent resistance to chemicals and UV radiation. Polyimide films provide high temperature resistance and are used to protect surfaces near hot areas on the aircraft. Lastly, "Others" category refers to any other types of protective films used, such as polyethylene or polyester films, each offering their unique properties for safeguarding aircraft interiors.</p></p>
