<p><ul><li>Micro</li><li>String</li><li>Central</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar On Grid Inverter market can be classified into three main types: micro, string, and central inverters. Micro inverters are installed on each individual solar panel, converting the DC power into AC power. String inverters are connected to a series of solar panels, converting the DC power from multiple panels into AC power. Central inverters are used for larger-scale solar installations, connecting to a string of solar panels and converting their DC power into AC power. Each type of inverter has its advantages and suitability based on the solar installation's size and complexity.</p></p>
