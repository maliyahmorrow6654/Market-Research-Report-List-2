<p><p>Soybean lecithin is a widely used food additive derived from soybeans. There are two main types of soybean lecithin: raw soybean lecithin and refined soybean lecithin. Raw soybean lecithin is obtained directly from soybean oil during its extraction process and contains impurities. On the other hand, refined soybean lecithin undergoes further processing to remove impurities and provide a more purified product. Both types are used in various food products as emulsifiers, stabilizers, or flavor enhancers, but refined soybean lecithin is preferred for its improved quality and purity.</p></p>
