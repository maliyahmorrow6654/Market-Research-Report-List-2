<p><strong>The Optoelectric Nuclear Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optoelectric Nuclear Battery market consists of two main types: Thermal Conversion Type and No-Thermal Conversion Type. </p><p>Thermal Conversion Type batteries utilize heat generated from nuclear decay reactions to convert it into electricity. This type of battery operates by employing a thermoelectric material that generates a voltage difference when exposed to heat.</p><p>On the other hand, No-Thermal Conversion Type batteries use radiation emitted during nuclear decay to directly produce electricity. These batteries employ photovoltaic materials that convert the photons released by the nuclear decay into electricity.</p><p>Both types of batteries offer alternative solutions for generating electricity using nuclear energy, with thermal conversion focusing on heat-to-electricity conversion and no-thermal conversion utilizing direct radiation-to-electricity conversion.</p></p>
