<p><p>The residential lightning protection system market consists of three main types, namely lightning protection systems, earthing systems, and others. Lightning protection systems are designed to safeguard residential buildings from the damaging effects of lightning strikes by providing a safe path for electrical energy to the ground. Earthing systems are used to prevent electrical hazards by establishing a low-resistance path for fault current to flow into the ground. The "others" segment includes additional systems such as surge protection devices, grounding rods, and lightning arresters that enhance the overall residential lightning protection system.</p></p>
