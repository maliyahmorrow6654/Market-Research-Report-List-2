<p><p>Hot wire air flow sensors are electronic devices used to measure the amount of air flowing through a duct, pipe or any other enclosed space. They operate on a principle where a heated wire is cooled down by the air flow passing over it, and this change in temperature is measured to determine the velocity and volume of air flow. These sensors are commonly used in automotive applications to measure the intake air flow in engines.</p><p>The hot wire air flow sensors market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
