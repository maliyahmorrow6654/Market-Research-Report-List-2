<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Cutting mat market consists of two main types: hard-surface (self-sealing) mats and self-healing mats. Hard-surface mats are made of heavy-duty materials that do not self-heal but provide a durable cutting surface. They offer resistance to knife cuts and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, self-healing mats are constructed with a soft surface that closes up after being cut with a knife, preventing further damage. These mats are perfect for precise and delicate tasks such as crafting and sewing, as they provide a long-lasting and smooth cutting surface.</p></p>
