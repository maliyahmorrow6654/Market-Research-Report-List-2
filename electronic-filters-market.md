<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Band Pass Filter</li><li>Band Reject Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic filters are devices used to allow certain frequencies to pass through while attenuating or blocking others. Low pass filters let low frequencies pass and attenuate higher frequencies, commonly used in audio systems. High pass filters do the opposite, allowing high frequencies while attenuating low frequencies. Band pass filters permit a specific range of frequencies to pass through, useful in radio communication and signal processing. Conversely, band reject filters attenuate a specific range of frequencies and are often utilized to eliminate interference in electronic devices.</p></p>
