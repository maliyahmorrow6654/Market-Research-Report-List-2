<p><p>The staple guns market can be categorized into three types: manual staple guns, electrical staple guns, and pneumatic staple guns. Manual staple guns are operated by hand, requiring physical force to drive staples into surfaces. Electrical staple guns are powered by electricity, simplifying the task by automatically driving staples. Pneumatic staple guns use compressed air to drive staples and are commonly used in construction and woodworking industries. All three types serve different purposes but aim to provide efficient and convenient solutions for fastening materials.</p></p>
