<p><p>The vocal dynamic microphone market consists of two main types: wireless and wired microphones. Wireless microphones are equipped with a built-in transmitter that allows freedom of movement during performances or presentations. They rely on radio frequency technology to transmit the audio signal to a receiver. Wired microphones, on the other hand, are physically connected to the audio system through cables. They offer a reliable and direct connection but may restrict movement. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications, depending on the need for mobility or stability in audio transmission.</p></p>
