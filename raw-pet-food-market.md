<p><p>The raw pet food market consists of two main types: dry food and wet food. Dry food refers to the form of pet food that is dehydrated and has a low moisture content. It typically comes in the form of kibble, pellets, or biscuits. On the other hand, wet food has a high moisture content and is usually in the form of canned or pouch food. Both types of pet food offer different advantages, such as convenience and shelf life for dry food, and higher palatability and hydration for wet food.</p></p>
