<p><p>The exfoliating products market consists of two main types: exfoliators and scrubs. Exfoliators are products that use chemical agents, such as alpha or beta hydroxy acids, to remove dead skin cells and promote skin cell turnover. This type of product is often gentler on the skin and suitable for sensitive skin types. On the other hand, scrubs are physical exfoliants that contain granules or beads to physically scrub away dead skin cells. Scrubs are generally more abrasive and are typically used on less sensitive areas of the body, such as elbows or feet.</p></p>
