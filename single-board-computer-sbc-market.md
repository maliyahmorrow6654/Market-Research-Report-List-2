VME (Versa Module Eurocard) boards are widely used in military and aerospace sectors for their ruggedness and reliability. VPX (Versa Module Eurocard Extensions for Instrumentation) boards offer high-speed data transfer and are commonly utilized in defense and aerospace industries. ATCA (Advanced Telecom Computing Architecture) boards provide scalable and reliable solutions for telecommunication applications.</p></p>
