<p><p>The micro actuator market can be classified into various types based on the technology used. Electric micro actuators are driven by electrical energy and are commonly used in applications that require precise positioning or movement. Functional material-based micro actuators use special materials that change their shape or properties when exposed to certain stimuli such as heat or light. Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) micro actuators utilize alloys that have the ability to remember their original shape and can be triggered to return to that shape. Other types of micro actuators include pneumatic, hydraulic, and piezoelectric actuators, among others. These actuators find applications in industries such as robotics, healthcare, automotive, and aerospace, enabling precise and controlled motion in small-scale systems.</p></p>
