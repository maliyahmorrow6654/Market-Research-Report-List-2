<p><p>Nachos are a popular snack that come in two main market types: fried and baked. Fried nachos are made by frying corn tortilla chips until they become crispy and golden, giving a rich and indulgent flavor. Baked nachos, on the other hand, are prepared by baking the tortilla chips in the oven, resulting in a lighter and healthier option. Both types offer a delicious base for various toppings like melted cheese, salsa, guacamole, or sour cream. The choice between fried and baked nachos depends on personal preferences and dietary considerations.</p></p>
