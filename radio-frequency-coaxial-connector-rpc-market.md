With the demand for compact and lightweight devices increasing, manufacturers are developing smaller connectors that offer high performance and reliability. These miniaturized RPCs are particularly suitable for applications in industries such as healthcare, consumer electronics, and automotive.</p><p>Another trend is the rapid development of RPCs with higher frequency ranges.
