<p><p>A transformerless UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) is an alternative to the traditional transformer-based UPS system. It eliminates the need for a bulky and heavy transformer, resulting in a more compact and lightweight design. Transformerless UPS systems use innovative technology such as capacitors, inductors, and power converters to perform the voltage conversion and regulation functions that were previously executed by transformers.</p><p>The transformerless UPS market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to several factors. One of the main drivers of market growth is the increasing demand for reliable power supply solutions across various industries. The transformerless UPS systems provide better power quality, efficiency, and reliability compared to traditional transformer-based systems.</p><p>Moreover, the transformerless UPS systems offer advantages such as compact size, reduced weight, and lower installation and maintenance costs. These factors make them an attractive option for data centers, healthcare facilities, industrial applications, and commercial buildings, where space and cost savings are essential considerations.</p><p>Additionally, the rising focus on reducing carbon footprint and energy consumption has also led to the adoption of transformerless UPS systems.
