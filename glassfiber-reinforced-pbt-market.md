<p><p>The Glassfiber Reinforced PBT market consists of two main types: Pure PBT/Glass Fiber Compounds and Glass-fiber Reinforced PBT Blends. Pure PBT/Glass Fiber Compounds refer to PBT resin mixed with glass fiber, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties such as improved strength and stiffness. On the other hand, Glass-fiber Reinforced PBT Blends involve a combination of PBT resin with other materials like polycarbonate or polyethylene, creating a blend with unique characteristics and improved performance in specific applications.
