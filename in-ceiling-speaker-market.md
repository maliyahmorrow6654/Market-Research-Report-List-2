<p><p>The In-Ceiling Speaker market can be classified into two main types: Active In-Ceiling Speakers and Passive In-Ceiling Speakers. Active In-Ceiling Speakers are self-powered units that have built-in amplifiers, eliminating the need for an external receiver. They are easier to install and offer convenience in terms of controlling volume and audio settings. On the other hand, Passive In-Ceiling Speakers require an external amplifier or receiver to power them and adjust audio settings. They may require additional wiring but offer more flexibility in terms of customization and compatibility with existing audio systems.</p></p>
